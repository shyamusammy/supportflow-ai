Customer Support Guide

Section 1
A customer may contact support regarding billing issues.
Duplicate charges should be investigated immediately.
Refunds must be processed within 5 business days.

Section 2
Login issues are among the most common support requests.
Users should reset passwords before escalation.
Multi-factor authentication problems should be reviewed.

Section 3
Subscription management includes upgrades, downgrades and cancellations.
Changes should be reflected immediately.

Section 4
Security incidents require urgent attention.
Account compromise must be escalated.
Unauthorized access should be investigated.

Section 5
Technical incidents should be categorized by severity.
Critical outages require executive notification.
